== Description ==

<h4>Ni WooCommerce Order Delivery</h4>
<p>
Ni WooCommerce Order Delivery empowers your e-commerce store with customizable order delivery dates directly on the checkout page, allowing customers to select their preferred delivery dates hassle-free. The chosen delivery dates are seamlessly integrated into the admin edit page, order emails, and reports, ensuring clear communication and streamlined order processing.
</p>
<p>
With flexible settings, you can effortlessly manage the start and end delivery days, as well as select available delivery days to suit your business requirements. Gain valuable insights with comprehensive reporting, presenting key order details such as billing information, order totals, and delivery dates. The built-in export and filter options further enhance your administrative capabilities.
</p>
<p>
Additionally, the intuitive dashboard provides invaluable sales analysis, including today's sales, monthly trends, and yearly performance. Stay ahead of upcoming orders with insights into the next order delivery count and total. Ni WooCommerce Order Delivery optimizes your store's efficiency and enhances the customer experience, driving growth and satisfaction.
</p>




<blockquote>
<h4>Ni WooCommerce Order Delivery Key Features</h4>
<ul>
<li>Dashboard show the key sales analysis information.</li>
<li>Provide the next or upcoming order delivery list.</li>
<li>Sales order list provide the order details information with filter and order export option.</li>
<li>Setting option to manage the Ni WooCommerce Order Delivery functionality</li>
<li>Enable disable Order Delivery date.</li>
<li>Mange start and end delivery days.</li>
<li>Option to select delivery day.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
</blockquote>
